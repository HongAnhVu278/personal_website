Fractal Trees
-------------------
An interactive creative-coding project that uses L-systems and turtle graphics to visualize different emotional states as generative fractal trees.

The project explores how abstract emotions can be encoded as system parameters( grammar, angles, recursion depth, and motion) rather than narrative or metaphor.
-------------------
Tech: p5.js
